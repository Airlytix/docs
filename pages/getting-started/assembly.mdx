  ### Printing Recomendations

  - Material: White PLA
  - Layer Height: 0.2mm
  - Walls: 3
  - Infill: 15%
  - Supports: Yes, except for front

## Assembly Steps

1. Press the light guide into the case front
2. Attach the connector cable to the PCB
3. Place the PCB into the case front and secure with the M1.6x4mm screw
4. Connect the other end of the connector cable to the sensor module
5. Place the sensor module into the case body
6. Screw together the case front and back using the two M1.6x20mm screws
7. Place the wall mount into the back of the case
