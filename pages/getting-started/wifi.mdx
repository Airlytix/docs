    1. **Power Up Your Device**: Plug your Airlytix sensor into a USB power supply.
    2. **Connect to the Hotspot**: On your device, navigate to WiFi settings and search for a network named "airlytix-es1-xxxxxx". Connect to this network.
    3. **Access the Setup Page**: A setup page should automatically open in your browser. If it doesn't, go to 192.168.4.1.
    4. **Enter WiFi Credentials**: On the setup page, select your home WiFi network and input the password.
    5. **Complete the Connection**: Click "Connect" to join your home WiFi network. Your Airlytix will restart to finalize the connection.

    You are done when the device reports it has connected and then restarts. After it joins your home WiFi, it will be available for discovery in Home Assistant.

    If the device reports an error when connecting, verify that you are using the correct 2.4 GHz network name and password, then try again.
